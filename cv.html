About Me

Hi there! I'm Gift Mthombeni, a Final Year Electrical Engineering student at Cape Peninsula University of Technology (CPUT) and a passionate tech enthusiast. My interests lie in energy studies, with a strong curiosity about emerging technologies, particularly in renewable energy—including wind turbines and hydrogen production.

My journey into engineering and technology began with a deep fascination for energy systems and how innovation can enhance their efficiency. Over time, this curiosity evolved into a passion for developing impactful solutions. Whether it's building AI-driven applications and deploying them seamlessly in the cloud or analyzing energy data for optimization, I strive to merge technical expertise with innovation. AI and machine learning play a significant role in my approach, bridging the gap between smart technology and energy efficiency.

My primary research interests revolve around energy efficiency and hydrogen production. After graduation, I plan to register as a Candidate Professional Engineering Technologist while pursuing a postgraduate diploma in either Power Plant Engineering or Smart Grid Technology.

Outside of engineering and coding, I enjoy exploring Cape Town’s beautiful beaches and discovering new coffee spots—definitely my favorite way to unwind!
