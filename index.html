About Me

I’m Gift Mthombeni, a final-year Electrical Engineering student at CPUT who enjoys turning real-world problems into reliable, well-documented solutions. My work spans modeling and simulation, embedded/controls, and modern web development—so I’m comfortable moving between lab, code, and production environments as the project requires.

Recent projects include building data-driven dashboards and full-stack tools, developing automation/monitoring features, and shipping clean user interfaces. I use Node.js, React, MySQL, Firebase, and REST APIs alongside engineering toolchains such as MATLAB/Simulink. I apply AI pragmatically to speed up research, testing, and documentation—not as the end product.

I bring strong fundamentals, version control with Git/GitHub, end-to-end debugging, and a focus on clear requirements, measurable outcomes, and maintainable code. I’m adaptable and open to roles across engineering, software, and data where I can learn quickly, collaborate well, and deliver impact.

When I’m not building, I’m usually exploring new techniques, reading docs, or refining small UX details that make systems easier to use.
